Frequently asked questions

Does the app connect to my Pokémon GO account?
No. The app has no login and never connects to Niantic servers. It only generates search strings that you copy and paste manually.

Why does the app never say "safe to delete"?
By design. The final search shows transfer candidates — Pokémon worth reviewing. You always decide. The app would rather leave clutter behind than put a keeper in your review list.

A search string returned nothing or looked wrong.
Pokémon GO search syntax occasionally changes. Email us the exact string and what you expected — string fixes ship quickly.

Can the app find exact PvP ranks (Top 100 etc.)?
No, and it never claims to. Pokémon GO search only exposes broad IV buckets, so PvP strings are review helpers, not exact rank guarantees.

I already have my own tags. Do I have to redo everything?
No. Add your existing tags on the Create Tags screen and they become extra exclusions in the final cleanup string — in addition to the SAFE tags, never instead of them.

Does the app have ads?
No ads, ever.

Something I tagged or favorited showed up as a transfer candidate.
That should never happen — it's the app's core promise. Email us immediately with the string and a screenshot.
