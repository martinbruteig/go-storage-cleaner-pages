Privacy Policy — GO Storage Cleaner

Last updated: 2026-06-13

The short version

We collect nothing. GO Storage Cleaner has no accounts, no login, no analytics, no ads, no tracking and no servers.

What the app does

GO Storage Cleaner generates Pokémon GO search strings locally on your device. You copy them manually and paste them into Pokémon GO yourself. The app never connects to Niantic servers, never accesses your Pokémon GO account and never reads your Pokémon inventory.

Data stored on your device

The app stores a small set of preferences locally on your device only:

  • your selected playstyles and cleanup mode
  • your PvP league and protection depth choices
  • your XP and Raid IV protection choices
  • tags you add for extra exclusion
  • whether you completed the tag setup

This data never leaves your device. Deleting the app deletes this data.

Data we collect

None.

  • No personal data
  • No account data
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No third-party SDK tracking

Clipboard

When you tap a Copy button, the generated search string is placed on your clipboard so you can paste it into Pokémon GO. The app does not read your clipboard.

Purchases and ads (future versions)

This version contains no payments, no ads and no ad SDKs. If purchases are added in a future version, payment processing will be handled by Apple and this policy will be updated before that release. The app will always be ad-free.

Children

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.
