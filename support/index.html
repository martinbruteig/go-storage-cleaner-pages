What GO Storage Cleaner does

GO Storage Cleaner helps Pokémon GO players review storage cleanup candidates more carefully.

The app generates conservative search strings that you copy and paste into Pokémon GO yourself: first to find and tag Pokémon worth keeping, then to review transfer candidates.

The workflow is simple:

Protect first. Clean second.

What it does not do

GO Storage Cleaner does not:

  • require login
  • access your Pokémon GO account
  • access Niantic servers
  • automate gameplay
  • transfer Pokémon for you
  • make exact PvP rank claims

Search strings are review helpers based on broad in-game filters and IV buckets. You always review results manually before transferring anything.

Basic troubleshooting

A search string returned nothing or looked wrong.

Pokémon GO search syntax may change over time. Email us the exact string, what you expected, and what happened. We will review it.

The Copy button did not work.

Long-press the search string text and copy it manually. Please email us your app version, which is shown in Settings.

Something I tagged or favorited showed up as a transfer candidate.

This is a high-priority safety issue. Please email us immediately with the exact search string and, if possible, a screenshot.
