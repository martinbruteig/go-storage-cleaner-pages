Privacy Policy — GO Storage Cleaner

Publisher: Aksjefokus AS · Last updated: 2026-06-13

The short version

GO Storage Cleaner does not collect personal data through the app.

GO Storage Cleaner is free to download and offers optional In-App Purchases for Pro features.

What the app does and does not do

GO Storage Cleaner does not require login.

The app does not:

  • access Pokémon GO accounts
  • access Niantic servers
  • automate gameplay
  • use a backend in the current version
  • use analytics in the current version
  • show ads in the current version
  • collect personal data through the app

GO Storage Cleaner generates Pokémon GO search strings locally on your device. You copy them manually and paste them into Pokémon GO yourself.

In-App Purchases

GO Storage Cleaner offers optional In-App Purchases for Pro features.

Payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. GO Storage Cleaner and Aksjefokus AS do not receive or store your payment card details.

Apple may process payment-related information according to Apple's own terms and privacy policy.

The app may store purchase entitlement status locally on your device so Pro features can be unlocked after purchase or restored later.

Purchase restoration is handled through Apple's App Store purchase systems.

Data stored on your device

User preferences, such as selected playstyles, cleanup mode, protection choices and tags you add, are stored locally on your device only.

Purchase entitlement status may also be stored locally on your device.

This data is not sent to Aksjefokus AS.

Deleting the app removes local app data stored by the app on that device.

Clipboard

The clipboard is only used when you tap Copy. The generated search string is placed on the clipboard so it can be pasted into Pokémon GO.

The app does not read the clipboard.

Support contact

If you contact Aksjefokus AS by email, we will receive your email address and any information you choose to include in your message.

Support emails are used only to respond to support requests, investigate issues and improve the app.

We do not use support emails for marketing.

Future changes

The current version does not use ads or analytics.

If ads, analytics, backend services, account features or third-party purchase management services are added in a later version, this privacy policy will be updated before that release.
