Privacy Policy — GO Storage Cleaner

Publisher: Aksjefokus AS · Last updated: 2026-06-13

The short version

GO Storage Cleaner does not collect personal data.

What the app does and does not do

  • GO Storage Cleaner does not require login
  • The app does not access Pokémon GO accounts
  • The app does not access Niantic servers
  • The app does not automate gameplay
  • The app does not use a backend in the current version
  • The app does not use analytics in the current version
  • The app does not show ads in the current version
  • The app does not collect personal data

The app generates Pokémon GO search strings locally on your device. You copy them manually and paste them into Pokémon GO yourself.

Data stored on your device

User preferences (selected playstyles, cleanup mode, protection choices and tags you add) are stored locally on the device only. Deleting the app deletes this data.

Clipboard

The clipboard is only used when the user taps Copy — the generated search string is placed on the clipboard so it can be pasted into Pokémon GO. The app does not read the clipboard.

Future changes

If ads, analytics or payments are added in a later version, this privacy policy will be updated before that release.
